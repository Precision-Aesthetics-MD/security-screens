What is security screen?

A security screen offers the same benefits of a safety screen however are specifically engineered to keep intruders out. Everything about the screen is designed with security in mind, from the stainless steel construction, the installation process, to the frame, hinges and locking system. Most security screen door frames are constructed of steel, aluminum or a metal alloy.. If steel security screen doors are properly treated and powder coated, they should not show signs of rust for at least eight to 10 years. They then can be recoated.The frame can be steel or aluminium. Industry experts say a steel door (if properly constructed and installed) is regarded as the most effective security screen door. If a steel door meets the Standard it'll be protected against corrosion. Aluminium is less prone to corrosion than steel.
